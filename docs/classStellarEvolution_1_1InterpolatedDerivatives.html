Create an object that interpolates derivatives from evolution tracks.

The input grid of derivatives may be of quantity of log(quantity) vs. age or log(age). The returned derivatives are always of quantity vs age (no log of anything).

Parameters
massThe stellar mass at which to evaluate the interpolated derivatives.
fehThe stellar [Fe/H] at which to evaluate the interpolated derivatives.
derivativesThe derivatives at each grid intersection.
interp_massesThe masses of the grid intersections.
interp_fehThe sorted [Fe/H] of the grid intersections.
ageIf not NaN, derivatives are assumed to calculate derivatives w.r.t. ln(age), whereas this object always returns derivatives w.r.t. age.
log_quantityAre derivatives of log(quantity) instead of quantity?
delete_derivativesShould derivatives be deleted when this object is destroyed?
